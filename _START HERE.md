# Welcome

Welcome to your Obsidian vault. 

## Important Commands

- New note: `⌘N`
- Open command palette: `⌘⇧P`
- Open quick switcher: `⌘P` 
- Open settings: `⌘,`

Go to `Settings->Hotkeys` to see all hotkeys and add your own.
